Made for the iPad

We have jumped into iBooks Author with both feet and created an interactive iBook that strives to be more than just another cheap ebook. We aim to transport you to New Zealand and give you a true sense of what life is like in this far off world. With over 60 minutes of video interviews and tours, photo galleries, quizzes, and more; this just ain’t your grandma’s kindle.

Meet the Authors

Hello from the Dalton family! We recently realized a life long dream and immigrated to New Zealand from the United States. During the almost year long process of our immigration we realised it is really hard to find good information on New Zealand. Other than a few expat forums and blogs we felt we had to really dig to find anything current about immigrating to, and then living in, New Zealand.

This research was fundamentally important to us because we moved to New Zealand without ever having visited! We were following an inner calling and really had to trust our instincts and do a metric ton of research to build up to the decision to sell everything we own and immigrate.

Once we settled and found our home we set ourselves to creating this iBook so that anyone coming after will have all the benefit of the things we learned along the way.
